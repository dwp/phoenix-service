Get expert health support at work for a disabled employee or an employee with a health condition

Use this service to find subsidised workplace health services. These are often referred to as occupational health services.

If an employee's condition is affecting them at work, use this service to find experts who can:

  • help you identify what support an employee needs in relation to a specific concern
  • give you advice on supporting an employee back to work if they have become ill

Who can use this service

You can use this service if:

  • you are a micro, small or medium business based in England or Wales
  • your yearly turnover is less than X

Eligible businesses will get an 80% discount when buying qualifying occupational health services.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your business PAYE number
  • your number of employees
  • your turnover for the tax year x
