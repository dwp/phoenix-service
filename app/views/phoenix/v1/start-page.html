Get expert health support at work for a disabled employee or an employee with a health condition

Use this service to find subsidised workplace health services if an employee's condition is affecting them at work. Workplace health services are often referred to as occupational health and can provide a range of support to employers. They are usually paid for, but can save you time and money by helping to keep an employee in work.

Eligible businesses will get an 80% discount when buying qualifying occupational health services.

This service will help you find subsidised occupational health experts who can:

  • give tailored advice about an employee’s specific health condition or impairment
  • decide on changes in the workplace
  • conduct workplace assessments
  • support your employee to manage their health

Who can use this service

You can use this service if:

  • your business is registered in England or Wales
  • you have less than 250 employees
  • you meet one of the following statements, you have:
    • 1-50 employees, a turnover less than £6.5 million or balance sheet total less than £3.26 million
    • 51-250 employees, a turnover less than £25.9 million or balance sheet total less than £12.9 million

Before you start

You will need:

  • your business PAYE number
  • your number of employees
  • your turnover or balance sheet for the tax year 2020-2021
