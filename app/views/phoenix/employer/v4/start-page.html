Get help to find and pay for an occupational health assessment

As a small or medium sized business, you may have concerns about your employee’s health.  This could be because of a disability, a health concern or long-term health condition.

This service can help you find an approved health care provider to carry out a comprehensive health assessment at an 80% discount. 

A health assessment can provide you with:  

  • support for a disabled person to stay in work
  • assistance for employees get back into work after being off sick
  • help to manage your employee’s physical and mental health
  • tailored advice to manage an employee’s health condition

Once carried out, you and your employee will get a report with recommended next steps. Any further occupational health services are not discounted.

Who can use this service

You are eligible if your business is registered in England or Wales and you have:

  • 1-50 employees and your annual turnover is £6.5 million or less or a balance sheet total of £3.26million or less

    • or

  • 51-250 employees, and annual turnover is £25.9million or less or a balance sheet total of £12.9million or less
