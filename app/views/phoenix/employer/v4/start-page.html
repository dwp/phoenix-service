Get discounted occupational health advice for an employee

Use this service to help you find and pay for an occupational health assessment for an employee.

You should consider an assessment if an employee is finding it difficult to work because of: 

  • an injury, such as a broken leg
  • a diagnosed or undiagnosed health concern 
  • a long-term health condition
  • an impairment or disability
  • their mental or physical health  

An assessment can help you understand what support an employee needs to stay in or return to work. This could include:

  • advice on any reasonable adjustments you could make 
  • guidance on free and paid-for changes you could make to support them
  • links to other organisations who may be able to help 
There's different information if you're an employee. Read how occupational health can help keep you healthy and safe at work

What you'll pay

The total cost of an occupational health assessment is between £100 and £400. Eligible businesses will get an 80% discount on the total cost. After discount, the amount you pay will be between £20 and £80. 

Any further occupational health services are not discounted.

Who can use this service

You are eligible if your business is registered in England or Wales and you have one of the following:

  • 1 to 50 employees, and your yearly turnover is £10.2 million or less, or your yearly balance sheet is £5.1 million or less
  • 51 to 250 employees, and your yearly turnover is £22.8 million or less, or your yearly balance sheet is £11.4 million or less

Before you start

You'll need your employer PAYE reference number - you will find this on letters from HMRC.

You do not need to provide any personal information about your employee at this stage.
