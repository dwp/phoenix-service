How the listed workplace health providers have been evaluated

All the workplace health providers listed in this service have had to go through a range of eligibility checks to prove that they:

  • have the required professional industry qualifications and governance procedures and policies
  • can deliver a quality, professional assessment

They must either be accredited with SEQOHS (opens in a new tab) (Safe, Effective Quality Occupational Health Service) or they must have shown us:

  • proof of registration to a professional body
  • evidence of clinical governance
  • evidence of proper complaints or feedback mechanisms
  • evidence of professional data management
  • evidence of a referral pathway

In addition, all workplace health providers have declared that they will:

  • ensure the person undertaking the workplace health assessment is, or has oversight from, a qualified professional
  • ensure there is a conversation or interaction with the employer before the assessment to understand the context of their business 
  • discuss with the employee during the assessment what will be included in the report and/or give the employee the option to see the report prior to sending it to their employer 
  • ensure the maximum cost for an assessment through this service will be £400
