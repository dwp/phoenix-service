How the listed occupational health providers have been evaluated

All the health providers listed in this service have had to go through a range of eligibility checks, and prove they meet certain professional standards.

These include:

  • proof of registration to a professional body
  • evidence of clinical governance
  • evidence of proper complaints or feedback mechanisms
  • evidence of professional data management
  • a referral pathway

In addition, all providers have declared that they will:

  • have an initial conversation with the employer to understand context of business 
  • discuss with the employee (during the assessment) about what will be included in the report 
  • ensure the person undertaking the assessment is, or has oversight from, a qualified professional
  • give the employee the option to see the report prior to sending to their employer
  • produce a comprehensive assessment report
