Get an 80% discount on an occupational health assessment for an employee

This service is primarily for employers. 

However, employees can also use it to find out how to get expert health support at work.

Employers can use this service to:

  • get an 80% discount code to help pay for an occupational health assessment for an employee
  • search for a private sector occupational health provider to do the assessment

What an occupational health assessment provides

You should consider an assessment for an employee if they're finding it difficult to work because of their health. This could include:

  • their mental or physical health
  • an injury, such as a broken leg 
  • a diagnosed or undiagnosed health concern 
  • a long-term health condition
  • an impairment or disability  

After the assessment, you and and your employee will get a report with advice. This can help you understand what support they need to stay in or return to work. This could include:

  • advice on any reasonable adjustments you could make 
  • guidance on free and paid-for changes you could make to support them
  • links to other organisations who may be able to help 

See guidance on how an occupational health assessment works.

What you'll pay

Eligible companies get an 80% discount on an occupational health assessment. 

The cost of the assessment depends on which provider you choose and ranges from £100 to £400. After discount, the amount you pay will be between £20 and £80. 

Any further occupational health services are not discounted.

Who can use this service

You are eligible if your business is registered in England or Wales and you have one of the following:

  • 1 to 50 employees, and your yearly turnover is £10.2 million or less, or your yearly balance sheet is £5.1 million or less
  • 51 to 250 employees, and your yearly turnover is £22.8 million or less, or your yearly balance sheet is £11.4 million or less

You can browse the list of providers without proving you're eligible.

Before you start

You'll need your employer PAYE reference number. You'll find this on letters about PAYE from HMRC. For example, '123/AB456'.

You do not need to provide any personal information about your employee at this stage.

What happens next

When you press start now, you will:

  • answer some questions to find out if you're eligible
  • receive a discount code you can redeem with a provider
  • search for an occupational health provider to do an assessment
