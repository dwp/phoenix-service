How the scheme works     

Employer proves eligibility and gets a discount code     

Employers can access the scheme through a GOV.UK service. After answering some questions to show they’re eligible for the scheme, they’ll get a unique discount code. They’ll use this to claim the 80% discount.

Employer searches for a provider     

They can then use the next part of the service to find a workplace health provider to do the assessment. They can search by filters according to their needs to get a list of potential assessment providers.  

Employer contacts a provider 

They can then contact whichever assessment providers they wish for an initial conversation about their employee’s health. Once satisfied with a provider they can arrange an assessment and will give the provider the discount code.  

Provider checks and claims code 

The provider then uses an online code-checker tool to check the code is valid and matches the organisation that has requested the assessment. They then claim the code for that assessment. 

Provider does assessment and claims payment 

The provider then carries out the assessment, invoices the client for 20% of the cost (up to the £400 assessment cost limit, and any extra travel costs over this limit) and uses the discount code to invoice DWP for the rest.   

Post-assessment survey 

After the assessment DWP may contact the employer for feedback about the quality the service received.
