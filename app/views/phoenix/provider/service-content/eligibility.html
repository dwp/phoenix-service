Eligibility   

There are different eligibility requirements, depending on whether your organisation is SEQOHS (Safe, Effective Quality Occupational Health Service) accredited or not. 

 

In general, to be accepted, we will need to be satisfied that you: 

  • have the required professional industry qualifications and governance procedures and policies 
  • can deliver a quality, professional assessment 

Evidence and declarations required     

For all organisations 

Any occupational health and vocational rehabilitation providers wanting to join the scheme will need to send us:

  • declarations about the service they provide
  • an example of a template or redacted report

The declarations are that:

  • there will be a conversation or interaction with the employer before the assessment to understand the context of their business 
  • they will have a conversation with the employee during the assessment to discuss what will be included in the report and/or the employee will be given the option to see the report prior to sending it to their employer  
  • the person undertaking assessments is, or has oversight from:  
    • an occupational health physician   
    • an occupational health nurse
    • an occupational therapist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation or return to work  
    • a physiotherapist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation, or return to work  
    • a psychologist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation, or return to work  
  • the maximum cost for an assessment through this service will be £400   
    •  

The example of a template or redacted report must show: 

 
  • a remote or in-person assessment of an employee  
  • the writing of a comprehensive report, to include:  
    • the answering of any employer or management concerns  
    • the employee’s capacity for employment  
    • advice on the current functional ability of employee  
    • objective and impartial advice  
    • onward referrals (if required)  
    • an opportunity for follow-up discussion  
    • a biopsychosocial approach  

If your organisation is SEQOHS accredited 

You will need to tell us your organisation name that is registered with SEQOHS. 

If your organisation is not SEQOHS accredited 

You will need to send us evidence that you meet our standards concerning: 

  • clinical governance procedures 
  • complaints procedures and feedback mechanisms 
  • data management policies 
  • referral pathways
