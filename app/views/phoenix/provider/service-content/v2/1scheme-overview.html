Scheme overview   

The Workplace Health Assessment Discount Scheme is new initiative by the government. It’s designed to help small and medium-sized employers assist their employees by finding and engaging with workplace-focused health providers.  

This guide is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Employers can use this service to: 

  • get an 80% discount code to help pay for a workplace health assessment for an employee 
  • search for a private sector workplace health provider to do the assessment 

Eligible occupational health and vocational rehabilitation providers with workplace-based experience can apply to join the scheme. If accepted, they’ll be listed in register in the service and will then be searchable by employers.  

The scheme is open to any organisation registered in England or Wales with one of the following: 

  • 1 to 50 employees, and a yearly turnover of less than £10.2 million or a yearly balance sheet of less than £5.1 million 
  • 51 to 250 employees, and a yearly turnover of less than £22.8 million or a yearly balance sheet of less than £11.4 million 

A pilot scheme is expected to be running from Spring 2023, but this could be subject to change. You’ll be notified well before the scheme is about to open to employers.
