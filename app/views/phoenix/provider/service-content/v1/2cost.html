Costs and pricing    

You will need to tell us your price range of the workplace health assessments that you intend to deliver through this scheme. This will depend on the method of delivery, the complexity of the case and who carries out the assessment.

You can charge up to £400.

There may be travel costs when an assessment is in-person. The employer will pay for any travel costs that take the assessment over £400. Any costs over £400 are not discounted by DWP.
