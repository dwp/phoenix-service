What you need to send and where to send it    

If you think are eligible and wish to apply to join the Workplace Health Assessment Discount Scheme as an assessment provider, you need to:   

  • tell us the name of the person sending the application
  • tell us the name of the workplace health provider making the application
  • collate all the necessary evidential documents you need to send us, label them with their respective categories, for example ‘Clinical governance procedures’ and attach them to an email. 
  • copy this ‘Declarations’ text into the body of the email and write ‘YES’ next to each one. 
    •  

      I declare that: 

    • - there will be a conversation or interaction with the employer before the assessment to understand the context of their business 
    • - they will have a conversation with the employee during the assessment to discuss what will be included in the report and/or the employee will be given the option to see the report prior to sending it to their employer  
    • - the person undertaking assessments is, or has oversight from:  
      • an occupational health physician   
      • an occupational health nurse
      • an occupational therapist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation or return to work  
      • a physiotherapist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation, or return to work  
      • a psychologist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation, or return to work  
    • - the maximum cost for an assessment through this service will be £400   
  • Send the email to whads@dwp.gov.uk 
   

Your eligibility documentation will be reviewed by a workplace health professional from DWP. You should be notified about the result of your application within 4 weeks.
